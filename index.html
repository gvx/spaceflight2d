SpaceFlight2D a simple space shooter.

SpaceFlight2D is an award-winning[1], not-so-small 2D space shooter, in which you fly to other planets and conquer the galaxy. It's by far my most popular project[2]. Features include dynamically created systems, enemy ships and a simple storyline.
